why we did the duck project

We think ducks are really cool! We also wanted to help any lonely souls out there because we are CS majors.

More seriously, we recognize that loneliness has been on the rise, and that it's associated with many mental health issues: depression and anxiety, among others. With our duck buddy, we hope we can add a little joy to someone's life.

FUN FACT: In the CS field we use rubber ducks that we explain things to! It's called "ducking" and its a great way to debug.

This website was made by the "what the duck" team for VandyHacks 2023.
